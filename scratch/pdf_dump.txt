The Recruiting Dilemma: Beyond Keyword Matching
Traditional ATS keyword-matching filters fail to find the right candidates. Recruiters are either swamped with irrelevant keyword-stuffed
resumes or miss top-tier candidates who don't list specific brand names. Our solution ranks candidates the way a human recruiter would:
assessing context, experience depth, and real-world availability.

Core Design Principles:
• Rigorous Technical Gating: Calculate actual years of AI/ML-specific experience rather than relying on profile-wide metrics.
• Disqualification of Profile Honeypots: Identify and immediately filter logical inconsistencies (e.g. 0-duration expert skills or impossible
startup timelines).
• Filtering Out Non-Product Gaps: Reject candidates with experiences exclusively in mass consulting and IT-service firms.
• Behavioral Modulation: Use real-time availability metrics (notice period, response speed, activity, location) as a tie-breaker, not a
replacement for talent.
